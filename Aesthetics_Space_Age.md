# Space Age GSAPP Website Design System

## Design Vision

A retro-futuristic website inspired by 1950s--1970s Space Age design,
Googie architecture, NASA graphics, Pan Am branding, and optimistic
visions of the future. The interface combines warm mid-century interiors
with cosmic imagery.

## Visual Identity

-   **Mood:** Optimistic, playful, futuristic, elegant
-   **Style:** Mid-century modern + retro futurism
-   **Keywords:** Space Age, Atomic Age, Googie, capsules, portholes,
    orbital graphics, geometric patterns

## Color Palette

  Role            Color
  --------------- -----------
  Deep Space      `#082440`

  Ochre           `#F3A543`

  White           `#FFFFFF`

## Typography

-   Primary: Space Grotesk
-   Uppercase headings
-   Wide letter spacing
-   Large bold project titles
-   Small navigation labels

## Layout

-   Capsule header
-   Two-column split layout
-   Left: project information
-   Right: illustrated "space window"

## Components

### Header

Rounded border, left/center/right alignment, uppercase navigation.

### Hero

-   Year
-   Large project title
-   Student name

### Content Card

Rounded rectangle with cream background for readable content.

### Button

Capsule shape, orange fill, hover lift and glow.

### Space Window

Contains: - Planet - Orbit lines - Stars - Orange geometric wallpaper
panel

## Graphic Language

-   Circles
-   Capsules
-   Rounded rectangles
-   Orbit rings
-   Starbursts
-   Geometric wallpaper patterns

## Motion

-   Gentle floating planet
-   Hover animations
-   Soft transitions

## Accessibility

-   High contrast between cream and navy
-   Large headings
-   Simple, consistent navigation

## Design Principles

1.  Rounded over sharp.
2.  Optimistic rather than dystopian.
3.  Limited palette.
4.  Large areas of negative space.
5.  Geometric forms repeated throughout.
6.  Retro illustration instead of photorealism.
